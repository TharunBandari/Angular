GAVS Technologies is a prominent global IT services company with headquarters in New York City, USA. Established in 1998, GAVS has emerged as a leading provider of digital transformation and automation solutions. Their service portfolio encompasses Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML), Cloud Services, Managed IT Services, Application Services, and Customer Experience Management. Notably, GAVS specializes in AIOps (Artificial Intelligence for IT Operations), leveraging AI and ML to enhance IT operations' efficiency and reliability. The company's proprietary AIOps platform, the Zero Incident Framework (ZIF), is designed to minimize IT incidents proactively.
GAVS caters to diverse industries, including healthcare, finance, retail, and technology. They are known for their innovation and research and development efforts, continually striving to remain at the forefront of technology solutions. With offices and development centers in India and the UK, GAVS Technologies has a global reach, making it a trusted partner for organizations seeking to modernize their IT infrastructure and improve customer experiences. Committed to corporate social responsibility, GAVS actively participates in initiatives that benefit communities and the environment. In summary, GAVS Technologies' relentless focus on innovation and AIOps-driven IT solutions has solidified its reputation as a key player in the ever-evolving world of digital transformation and technology services.
